## Lesson Group 1-4

### Early Learning Goals

- To identify weather conditions
- To describe what people are wearing
- To identify and name activities related to each season
- To trace the names of the seasons
- To express where one wants to go and do
- To name seasons and months of the year

### Vocabulary

weather, clothing, colors, seasons (winter, spring, summer, fall), play in the snow/sand/leaves, mountains, theme park, beach, campsite, swimming, hiking, skiing

### Language Models

- What's the weather like? It's hot and sunny. What's (Jimmy) wearing? He's wearing a T-shirt, shorts, socks and tennis shoes. What season is this? It's spring.
- Where does Dino want to go? He wants to go (to the beach). What does he like to do? He likes to ride on the roller coaster. What months are in the winter? December, January and February.

## Lesson Group 5-8

### Early Learning Goals

- To identify forms of transportation and transportation locations
- To talk about vacation plans and the seasons
- To tell time on the half hour
- To review and recognize numbers to 20
- To talk about buying tickets, departure times and choosing a seat
- To count to 100 by tens

### Vocabulary

plane, train, bus, taxi, car, airport, train station, bus station, taxi stand, bus ticket, time, seat, numbers to 20, flight, time, numbers 10 to 100, airport, bus station

### Language Models

- Where's Dino? He's at the airport. Where's he going? He's going to the beach. How's he getting there? By plane. When's Dino going to the beach? In the summer.
- What time is it? It's 2:30. What time does the bus leave? At 10:30. What's your seat number? (Seven). What's the flight number? Number 40.

## Lesson Group 9-12

### Early Learning Goals

- To identify vacation destinations, forms of transportation and lodging
- To describe vacation-related activities
- To identify and trace the words for vacation locations
- To plan a vacation and talk about packing for a trip
- To identify clothing needed for different kinds of weather

### Vocabulary

beach, mountains, plane, taxi, hotel, house, tent, cabin, vacation activities, weather-related clothing and items

### Language Models

- Where are you going to go on vacation? I'm going to go to the beach. What are you going to do at the beach? I'm going to swim.
- How do you want to get there? I want to go by plane. Where do you want to stay? I want to stay in a tent. How's the weather? It's cold. What's she taking? She's taking a coat and boots.

## Lesson Group 13-16

### Early Learning Goals

- To describe actions in the past
- To listen and follow a story
- To discuss the importance of helping each other
- To describe how they and other characters felt about a situation
- To understand the importance of working together as a family

### Vocabulary

put up a tent, went hiking, built a campfire, slept in a tent, rode (drove), fell down, helped, dropped, pulled back into boat

### Language Models

- They went camping. What did they do? They (put up a tent). They went camping. What happened? Little Brother fell down. Who helped him? Dad helped him.
- What are the dinosaurs doing? The dinosaurs are working together to put up a tent!
